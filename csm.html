College of Science and Mathematics

       The College of Science and Mathematics (CSM) was created on July 1, 1997 by virtue of the Board of Regent’s approval of a proposal by Dean Rogelio V. Cuyno.

The faculty and staff of the School of Agri-Systems and Environment (SASE) as well as the Natural Science and Mathematics Cluster of the College of Arts and

Sciences (CAS) were re-assigned to the new College. On the other hand, the Humanities and Social Sciences Clusters of the old College of Arts and Sciences

became the new College of Humanities and Social Sciences. The SASE and CAS were effectively dissolved with the formation of the new units. The Board’s

approval provided that a Director who will function as the Dean of the College would head CSM.
